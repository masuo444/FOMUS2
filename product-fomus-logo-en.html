About the FOMUS Brand

FOMUS's mission is to revive the traditional Japanese craft "masu" with 1300 years of history. Beyond just traditional crafts, we provide cultural value that blends into modern lifestyles.

This FOMUS Logo Masu is an item that most symbolically expresses such brand identity. The logo carved on your chosen size tells the story of the fusion of Japanese aesthetics and modern design.
