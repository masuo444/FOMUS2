KUKU

Connecting the world through the power of culture and storytelling

KUKU is a global IP project developed by FOMUS that fuses traditional Japanese culture with contemporary creativity.
Spanning art, craft, animation, books, and technology, it creates diverse products and experiences centered on storytelling.

This project goes beyond mere content creation, aiming to create an "open creative space" where people worldwide can participate, support, and cultivate culture together.

Project Vision

"Creating a new cultural ecosystem that continues for a thousand years."

In the KUKU story, Japan's iconic traditional craft "Masu" also appears as an important element.
This Masu connects with FOMUS's craft line, functioning both as art and as a symbol of culture.

With this story as our axis, we combine:
– Japanese spirituality and aesthetic sensibility
– World-class craftsmanship
– Cutting-edge technology and market access based in Dubai

These elements expand into picture books, anime, AI, NFTs, goods, educational content, licensing business, and more.
Stories and products circulate together, building a sustainable model for both cultural and economic aspects.
