Design Concept

Reconstruction of Tradition: Based on the 1300-year-old masu form, we propose new aesthetics adapted to contemporary lifestyles.

Cultural Bridge: We aim to create works loved across borders by fusing Japanese aesthetics with contemporary art.

Pursuit of Functional Beauty: We express true functional beauty that combines decorative beauty with practicality.
